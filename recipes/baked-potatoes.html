Backed potatoes

Description

Easy and quick baked potatoes!

    Ingredients
  • potatoes
  • garlic
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • salt and pepper
    •
      Steps:
  • Place the potatoes into a large pot with water to cover; cook and stir garlic until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
  • Bring to a boil and cook over medium-high heat for about 10 minutes, or until tender.
  • Drain potatoes and toss with olive oil, black pepper, rosemary and salt to taste.
  • Place the potatoes skin-side down over indirect heat on the grill and reserve liquid. Grill for about 15 minutes.
  • Remove potatoes to a serving plate and sprinkle with the reserved olive oil mixture.
